About Me

Hey, I’m Mia, an aspiring game developer with a strong passion for storytelling, worldbuilding and visual design. I’m especially interested in how games can create meaningful player experiences through a combination of narrative, mechanics and atmosphere. I have experience developing both 2D and 3D games using Unity and C#, working on a range of solo and collaborative projects.

Alongside development, I’ve built skills across multiple areas of game creation. I work with tools such as Aseprite for pixel art and animation, Blender and Nomad Sculpt for basic 3D modelling and Ibis Paint for detailed digital artwork. I also have experience with level design through greyboxing and tilemapping, as well as playtesting and refining games based on feedback. I use GitHub for version control and Visual Studio for scripting and debugging, helping me stay organised and efficient when working on projects. Beyond technical skills, I value creativity, teamwork and adaptability. I enjoy collaborating with others, sharing ideas and taking responsibility within a group to help bring a project together. At the same time, I’m self-motivated and disciplined when working independently, always aiming to improve and learn from each project I complete.

Beyond Game Development

Outside of development, I’m passionate about art, writing and analysing games, films and media. I enjoy creating characters and worlds, exploring visual storytelling through photography, and finding inspiration in everyday environments. All of these interests feed back into my work, helping me create more thoughtful and immersive game experiences. I’m currently continuing to develop my skills and build my portfolio, with the goal of becoming a well-rounded game developer who can contribute both creatively and technically to future projects.
